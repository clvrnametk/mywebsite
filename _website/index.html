Hi, I’m Mike

At my core, I’m a designer. I have a passion for team building, teaching and mentoring.

Currently, I’m a lead UX instructor at GA where I teach and mentor young designers. Previously, I was VP of UX & Design at Reserve, a hospitality technology and payments platform.

As a leader...

I am a coach, mentor, and a champion for the power of design to facilitate positive outcomes. I bridge the gap between business and design focused viewpoints through empathy and communication.

As a designer...

The forest through the trees; imagine the product through the vision. I’m an unwavering user advocate and design champion.
