Hi, I’m Mike

I am a design leader and a veteran of the NYC startup community with a passion for solving complex problems, building intelligent products, and assembling killer design teams.

Currently I am implementing user-centered design solutions at TodayTix and shaping the next generation of young minds as a lecturer at Pratt Institute. Previously, I led product and design initiatives as the VP of UX & Design at Reserve and as the Creative Director at MakerBot.
